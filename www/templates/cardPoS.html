How to Pay using Debit/Credit card or PoS ?

You might have used your Debit or Credit cards for payment but if not yet, then here's how you can use it.

You can pay via your card at any Shop having PoS Machine. The Machine looks exactly like the one in above picture.

Steps to Pay via Credit/Debit Cards.

  1. Insert or Swype your card in PoS Machine.
  2. Insert your PIN when asked.
  3. Get the Payment receipt.

Note: Please do not share your Credit/Debit card Pin with anyone for Security reasons.
