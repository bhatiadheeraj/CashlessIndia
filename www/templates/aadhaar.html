How to pay via Aadhaar Card ?

You can now pay to anyone via your Aadhaar Card.

You can do following things with Aadhaar card based Payment:

  1. Funds Transfer.
  2. Balance Enquiry.
  3. Cash Deposit or Withdrawal.
  4. Inter-Bank Transactions.

Note: To do payments through your Aadhaarcard link your Aadhaar Card to your bank account and you're ready to pay anywhere with just your Fingerprints.
